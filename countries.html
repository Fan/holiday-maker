France

Paris

Paris is very much worth visiting compared to other French cities. It's incredibly unique in that it is the capital of Paris, but also arguably the fashion and cultural capital of the world. If you want a French city break where you can sip wine in cafes, and explore the museums Paris is for you. Paris is known as an expensive city, but the cost of a trip to Paris can vary depending on your budget and travel style.
